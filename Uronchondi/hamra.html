Al Hamra Shopping City

Al-Hamra Shopping City is a project of Al-Hamra International Limited. The 12 storey shopping mall is situated at the center of Sylhet. It is one of the biggest shopping center in Sylhet. It includes four floors of centrally air-conditioned shopping space designed to make shopping a pleasure, fun and a great experience. The shops inside are well stocked and display international brands and labels. One can find lot of specialized shops specially of cloths, jewelries and crockery in the mall. The office of British Council and all the branches of all major share brokerage houses are situated here.
