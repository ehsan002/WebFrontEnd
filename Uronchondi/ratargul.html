Ratargul Swamp Forest

Madhabkunda waterfall is one of the largest waterfalls in Bangladesh. It is situated in Barlekha thana in Moulvi Bazar District, Sylhet Division. The waterfall is a popular tourist spot in Bangladesh. Big boulders, surrounding forest, and the adjoining streams attracts many tourists for picnic parties and day trips.
