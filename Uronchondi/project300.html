Paharpur

Architecturally and historically Paharpur Vihara is a treasured heritage of the world. It has been identified from a set of inscribed clay seals as the reputed Somapura Vihara.

Sundarban

Sundarbans is the world's biggest mangrove forest the home of the Royal Bengal tiger. These dense mangrove forests are criss-crossed by a network of rivers and creeks.

Rangamati

Rangamati is a favourite holiday destination because of its beautiful landscape, scenic beauty, lake, colourful indigenus groups, its flora and fauna, indigenous museum etc.
